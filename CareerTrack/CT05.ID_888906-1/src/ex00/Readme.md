**Exercise 00 – Before Starting an Internship/Job (Google)**

1. How do employees dress? Is there a dress code?
    - Most employees wear casual clothes like jeans and T-shirts. There is no strict dress code.

2. What should you wear on the first day to fit in?
    - Smart casual is a safe choice. For example, a clean shirt, comfortable pants, and simple shoes.

3. How will you know what time to arrive and leave?
    - You will get an onboarding email with your schedule. Usually, working hours are flexible between 9 am and 6 pm.

4. What else do you need to know about the workplace and working hours? (Write 5 points)
    - How to get your badge and laptop.
    - Where the cafeteria and coffee areas are.
    - How breaks and lunch work.
    - How to access systems and tools.
    - If remote work is possible and how to arrange it.

5. Make a list of questions you couldn't find answers to that you will ask on the first day.
    - What are the team’s expectations for my first month?
    - How do you measure success and performance?
    - Which communication tools do we use?
    - What training can I join to grow my skills?
    - Can I work remotely sometimes?
