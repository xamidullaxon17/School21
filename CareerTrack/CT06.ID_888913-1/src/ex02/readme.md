1. **What actions can you take to build pleasant relationships with your coworkers?**
    - Say hello and smile every day.
    - Help coworkers if they ask for support.
    - Have lunch or coffee together to talk and get to know each other.

2. **What should you do if a relationship with an important coworker doesn’t work out?**
    - Talk to them calmly and ask how you can work better together. 
    - Be polite and professional even if you don’t feel close.
    - Ask your manager for advice to improve the situation.

3. **What should you do if you have a conflict with a coworker?**
    - Speak privately and explain your feelings in a respectful way.
    - Listen to their side and try to understand their opinion.
    - If you can’t solve it, ask your manager or HR to help mediate.
